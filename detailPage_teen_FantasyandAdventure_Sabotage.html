"It does not matter if you are male or female... anyone from the ages of teen to adult would love this story...Tales by Sherrilyn Kenyon are major awesomeness!" -Huntress Reviews

The world of the Dark-Hunters is unlike anything you've ever seen before. It's dark. It's gritty. It's dangerous...

And it's a whole lot of unexpected action and snarky comebacks.

My name is Nick Gautier and I was born to end the world.

Or so I thought.

For years, I believed my destiny was beyond my control. But the Fates love to play with folks and I seem to be their favorite punchline.

Just when My crew and I thought we were done with the Apocalypse, the Fates had dragged back for another round. Now, we have to stop the Powers-That-Be and my future son or the ones I love and cherish will become memories.

It's a demon-eat-demon world and we are all the stands between humanity and total subjugation.

About the Author

Sherrilyn Kenyon is a regular at the top of bestseller lists in every genre in which she writes, including romance, young adult, and science fiction. More than sixty million copies of her books are in print in more than one hundred countries. Her series include the Dark-Hunter series, the League series, and the young-adult series, Chronicles of Nick. The Dark-Hunter and Chronicles of Nick series have both been adapted for film.
