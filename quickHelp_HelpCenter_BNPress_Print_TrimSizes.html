To set your trim and page sizes in Microsoft Word, follow the instructions below, also provided on the Microsoft Support page here.

Select the Page Design tab.

Page Setup group on the Page Design tab.

In the Page Setup group, select Size and click the icon that represents the page size that you want.

For example, click Letter (Portrait) 8.5 x 11". If you don’t see the size you want, either click

More Preset Page Sizes or click Create New Page Size to create a custom page size.

Interior Ink Qualities

Black &White – Most economical, printed on 50-lb paper in cream or white

Standard Color – Standard color quality, printed on 50-lb or 70-lb white paper

Premium Color – Highest color quality with more vivid color, printed on 70-lb white paper

Paper Color and Weights

Cream 50-lb – Available for B&W interiors, best suited for text-heavy books

White 50-lb – Available for B&W and standard color books

White 70-lb — Available for premium color options

Standard vs. Premium Color

Full Premium color books tend to be illustrated children’s book or a comic book with a lower page count.

Very expensive art books typically print using this option as well.

The photo and illustration output is going to be higher quality than the standard color.

Standard color books can be printed on 50- or 70-lb paper. 50-lb paper is ideal for books with some minor artwork but at a more economical price point than the thicker, 70-lb paper. Additional paper thickness is useful for art books, children’s books, or anything with heavy ink coverage, as the thicker paper will prevent bleed-through.
