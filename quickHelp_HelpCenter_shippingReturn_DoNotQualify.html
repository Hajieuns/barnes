Items That Do Not Qualify For Free Shipping

Not all items on BN.com qualify for Free Shipping. If an item does not have the Qualifies for Free Shipping indicator on its product page, shipping fees will be applied to the purchase of that item.

Items that do not qualify for Free Shipping include:

  • Gift Cards
  • Gift Certificates
  • Magazines
  • Digital products such as eBooks, SparkNotes, Quamut Charts, digital magazines, PDF files, and audiobook MP3s
  • Barnes & Noble Membership
  • Unusually sized or overweight items
  • Any other item not identified as eligible for Free Shipping.
